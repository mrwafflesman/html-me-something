My Cruise

Pictures and thoughts from my first cruise.

My cruise took place Summer 2016 and we went to multiple locations in the Carribean.

We started from Miami, Florida.

Our first stop was the U.S. Virgin Isles, where i bought a small model ship.

Our second stop was the British Virgin Isles, where we took a tour around the whole island.

Our third stop was Tortola, where we went to a famous whiskey distillery.
